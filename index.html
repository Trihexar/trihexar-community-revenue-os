Roadmap

From public identity to operational community infrastructure.

Phase 0

Foundation

Publish landing page, README, contribution rules, community narrative, and issue templates.

Phase 1

Community Activation

Launch Discord or Telegram, collect early contributors, define paid service offers, and document workflows.

Phase 2

Revenue Operations

Ship dashboards, sponsorship pages, partner pipeline, analytics, and client onboarding materials.

Phase 3

Protocol-Ready Layer

Prepare governance, treasury reporting, grant mechanisms, and token-safe documentation boundaries.
